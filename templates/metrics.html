Glossary

Metric Definitions

R² (Coefficient of Determination)

Proportion of variance in the target variable explained by the regression model. Range: 0 to 1. Higher is better.

MAE (Mean Absolute Error)

Average absolute difference between predicted and actual values. Expressed in the same units as the target (dollars).

Accuracy

Fraction of all predictions that are correct. Can be misleading for imbalanced datasets.

Precision & Recall

Precision: of predicted positives, how many are real. Recall: of real positives, how many were found. Trade-off controlled by threshold.

F1-Score

Harmonic mean of Precision and Recall. Preferred single metric when both false positives and false negatives matter.

AUC-ROC

Area under the ROC curve. Measures overall classifier quality across all decision thresholds. 1.0 = perfect, 0.5 = random.
